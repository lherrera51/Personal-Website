Experienced in creating innovative mechanical solutions for NASA and Ball Aerospace, from initial concepts to detailed 3D models and production-ready designs. Test Engineering

Experienced in supporting and executing complex testing procedures for the SDTS project at NASA JSC. Played a key role as operator and data analyst for docking tests, ensuring precision and reliablilty in capturing and interpreting critical performance metrics.

Developed as part of my academic thesis, this project involved designing a dynamic system model for the SDTS robot using Simulink.
